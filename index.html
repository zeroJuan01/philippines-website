Welcome to the Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelago comprising more than 7,000 islands, known for its rich biodiversity, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes.

Manila is the capital city, while Cebu and Davao are major urban centers.

From pristine beaches like Boracay and El Nido to the lush, mountainous regions of Banaue and Sagada, the Philippines has something for every traveler.
